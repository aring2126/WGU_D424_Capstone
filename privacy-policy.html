Privacy Policy for Goal Tracker

Effective Date: 10/21/25

Last Updated: 10/21/25

Introduction

Welcome to Goal Tracker ("the App"), a personal goal tracking application developed by Andrew Ring ("we," "us," or "our") as part of an academic capstone project at Western Governors University. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you use our app and tell you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you use Goal Tracker, you may provide the following information:

  • Goal titles and descriptions
  • Task titles and descriptions
  • Start and end dates for goals and tasks
  • Goal and task categories
  • Alert preferences and notification settings
  • Progress tracking information

Information Collected Automatically

The App collects minimal technical information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports and performance data (if the app crashes)

Information We Do NOT Collect

Goal Tracker does NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media files
  • Network or internet usage data
  • Financial information
  • Health or fitness data beyond goal titles you choose to enter

How We Use Your Information

All data in Goal Tracker is stored locally on your device only. We use your information solely to:

  • Display your goals and tasks within the app
  • Calculate progress and completion statistics
  • Generate reports based on your goal data
  • Send local notifications (if enabled) for goal reminders
  • Provide the core functionality of goal and task tracking

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

  • All your data is stored locally on your Android device using Room database
  • No cloud synchronization - your data never leaves your device
  • No external servers - we do not operate or use any external servers
  • No data transmission - your goal data is never transmitted over the internet

Security Measures

  • Data is stored in Android's application sandbox, protected by Android's security model
  • Each app installation has its own isolated data storage
  • Data is protected by your device's security features (lock screen, encryption)
  • Input validation and sanitization prevent malicious data entry

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data with anyone because:

  • All data remains on your device
  • We have no access to your data
  • No third-party services are integrated
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising networks are integrated

The only exception is when you explicitly choose to share a report using Android's share functionality, which is entirely under your control.

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

Data Access

  • View all your data directly within the app
  • Export your data via the Reports feature

Data Deletion

  • Delete individual goals or tasks at any time
  • Uninstalling the app completely removes all data
  • Clear app data through Android Settings to remove all information

Notifications

  • Control notification preferences within the app
  • Disable notifications through Android Settings at any time

Children's Privacy

Goal Tracker does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general use and does not target children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to the app, you can delete it by uninstalling the application.

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until:

  • You manually delete specific goals or tasks
  • You clear the app's data through Android Settings
  • You uninstall the application

We do not have access to your data and cannot retrieve it if deleted.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we may provide additional notice within the app.

We advise you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted.
